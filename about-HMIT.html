Hindu Institute of Management & Technolgy

The Hindu Institute of Management & Technology came into existence in 2001. Earlier MCA was started in 2001 and later with the efforts of worthy Management BCA was started in 2008 and MBA in 2010.
Our Campus away from the noise, pollution and crowd of the city, the campus of the institute has been developed at a distance of about 1.5 KM from the railway station Rohtak. The newly developed campus of the institute is located adjacent to Sh. L. N. Hindu College, Near Khushboo Garden Rohtak. We have been very specific in ensuring that our campus should have the healthy and pollution free environment and should also be easily accessible. Lush green surrounding of the campus are most conducive to the healthy academic environment for the students.
The institute is housed in a sprawling, pollution free campus with aesthetically constructed building having well ventilated and spacious classrooms, tutorial rooms, conference hall, four computer labs, fully computerized library, spacious well laid out canteen and developed playground for outdoor as well as indoor games.
